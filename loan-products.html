Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan products

What documents are required for a loan application?

You will need to provide a copy of your ID/Passport, recent payslips (last 3 months), bank statements (last 3 months), and proof of residence. Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type.

How long does the approval process take?

Most applications are processed within 24-48 hours. We strive to provide quick decisions so you can access funds when you need them most.

Who qualifies for a loan?

To qualify, you must be a Namibian citizen or permanent resident, 18 years or older, have a stable source of income, and meet our credit assessment criteria.

How does repayment work?

Repayments are made monthly through debit order from your bank account. You can choose a repayment period between 3 and 36 months depending on the loan type.

What is the maximum loan amount I can apply for?

The maximum loan amount depends on the loan type and your income. Personal loans range from N$ 5,000 to N$ 50,000, while SME loans can go up to N$ 200,000 for qualified applicants.
