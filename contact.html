Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to get a reply?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend contacting us via WhatsApp for faster response times.

Can I apply for a loan through WhatsApp?

Yes! You can start your loan application process through WhatsApp. Our team will guide you through the requirements and help you complete your application.

What documents do I need for a loan application?

You'll need a valid ID/Passport, recent payslips (last 3 months), bank statements (last 3 months), and proof of residence. Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type.

Do you offer weekend support?

Our office is open on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. For urgent matters outside these hours, you can reach us via WhatsApp and we'll respond as soon as possible.
