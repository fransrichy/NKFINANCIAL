Loan Requirements

Understanding our requirements ensures a smooth and fast loan application process. Make sure you have all necessary documents ready before applying.

What You Need to Apply

Prepare these documents and information to complete your loan application successfully

Required Documents Checklist

Valid ID / Passport

Government-issued identification document that is current and valid.

Latest Payslip

Your most recent payslip showing your income details.

Proof of Employment

Employment contract or letter from your employer confirming your employment status.

Bank Statements (1-3 months)

Recent bank statements to verify your financial activity and income deposits.

Contact Information

Current phone number and email address for communication.

Proof of Residence

Utility bill or rental agreement showing your current address (optional for some loans).

Document Details & Requirements

Understanding what we need and why helps ensure your application is processed quickly

Valid ID / Passport

Must be a government-issued identification that is current, valid, and clearly shows your photo and personal details.

Tip: Ensure all details are clearly visible in your copy

Latest Payslip

Should clearly show your name, employer details, income amount, and payment date. We need this to verify your income stability.

Tip: Provide your 3 most recent payslips for better assessment

Bank Statements

Recent statements (1-3 months) help us verify your income deposits, spending patterns, and financial stability.

Tip: Ensure statements show your name and account number clearly

Employment Proof

Employment contract, appointment letter, or recent letter from employer confirming your employment status and duration.

Tip: Self-employed? Provide business registration and financial statements

Proof of Residence

Utility bill (electricity, water), rental agreement, or municipal account not older than 3 months showing your current address.

Tip: The document must be recent and show your name and address

Guarantor Information

For certain loans, we may require a guarantor who meets our credit criteria and can provide similar documentation.

Tip: Discuss with your potential guarantor before applying

Before You Apply

Minimum age requirement: 18 years
Minimum monthly income: N$ 3,000
Namibian citizen or permanent resident
Valid bank account in your name
Stable employment (at least 3 months)
Good credit history
Application processing: 24-48 hours
Loan repayment terms: 3-36 months

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan requirements

Do I need a payslip to apply for a loan?

Yes, a recent payslip is required for employed applicants as it helps us verify your income and employment stability. For self-employed individuals, we require business registration documents and bank statements instead.

How long does the approval process take?

Most applications are processed within 24-48 hours after we receive all required documents. Complete applications with all necessary documentation are processed faster.

What is the maximum loan amount I can apply for?

The maximum loan amount depends on your income, credit history, and the specific loan product. Personal loans range from N$ 5,000 to N$ 50,000, while SME loans can go up to N$ 200,000 for qualified applicants.

Can self-employed people apply for loans?

Yes, self-employed individuals can apply. Instead of payslips, we require business registration documents, 6 months of bank statements, and financial statements to assess your income stability.

What if I don't have all the required documents?

We require all listed documents to process your application. If you're missing any documents, please contact us to discuss possible alternatives before applying.

Is a guarantor always required?

No, a guarantor is not always required. It depends on the loan amount, your credit history, and the specific loan product. Our team will inform you if a guarantor is needed during the application process.
